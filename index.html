How Ballast works

Three steps to inventory you can trust.

01

Link your stock & recipes

Set up shared pools (one stock → many variants) and bills of materials (a product made of components). A guided wizard does it in minutes — with a self-test that proves it's working.

02

Ballast watches every order

Real-time, idempotent tracking keeps your true available stock correct as orders, refunds, and cancellations happen. It never lets a pool go negative.

03

Checkout is blocked before you oversell

When a cart would exceed real stock, Ballast stops it at checkout — including Shop Pay, Apple Pay & Google Pay. The thing that breaks on Black Friday simply… doesn't.
